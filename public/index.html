Services

Fishing

Be prepared for unexpected downhole obstructions that could jeopardise your well. Our library of fishing solutions and equipment remove your roadblocks, minimise downtime, and swiftly gets you back on the critical path for your campaign.

View tools

  • Impact tools: Jars, intensifiers, bumper subs
  • External catch: overshots, box taps
  • Internal catch: spears, taper taps
  • Milling: Junk, taper, washover, and various mill designs with crushed carbide, TCI, and PDC
  • Junk recovery: fishing magnets, reverse circulation junk baskets
  • P&A: casing cutters, non-rotating spears, wellhead recovery systems
  • Mud motors
  • In-house customised fishing tools for complex challenges

Casing Exits

Save significant drilling cost by sidetracking from an existing well. Our whipstock, section mill, and pilot mill technologies offer premium cutters for enhanced performance and durability to reduce trips, opening a new path for you to explore.

View tools

  • Cased & open hole hydraulic or mechanical whipstock systems
  • Section mill and underreamers
  • Pilot mill
  • Swarf handling systems

Wellbore Cleanup

Prepare optimal surfaces for setting your well barriers and avoid leaks. Our wellbore clean up suite removes cement, scale, and other contaminants from the casing wall to enable your packers and plugs meet their best sealing capabilities over time.

View tools

  • Rotating and non-rotating casing scrapers and brushes
  • String magnets
  • Venturi systems
  • Jetting subs

Thru Tubing

Sometimes it is the small things that lead to massive problems. Our thru tubing fishing and milling solutions reopens access to your string, enabling new opportunities for well intervention and abandonment.

View tools

  • Overshots, spears, JDC and GS pulling tools
  • Impact tools: Jars, intensifiers
  • Milling: Junk, taper, and various mill designs with crushed carbide, TCI, and PDC
  • Mud motors
  • Motorhead assemblies, hydraulic disconnect
  • Hydraulic cutters
  • Macaroni BOPs
